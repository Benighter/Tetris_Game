    How To Play

    Clear full lines, keep the stack low, and survive as the speed ramps up.

    Keyboard: left and right move, up rotates, down soft drops, and space hard drops.

    Touch: drag the board to steer, tap to rotate, hold to soft drop, and flick down to hard drop.

    Gamepad: menus use A to confirm and B to go back. In game, X or B rotates, A or RB drops, and Start pauses.
